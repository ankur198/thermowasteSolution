Land turning into Landfills



Amount of waste generated has been rapidly increasing at the rate of 5% per annum since last decade. If it goes on like this without proper recycling solution, then according to reports by ASSOCHAM, India will have to set aside 88 sq. km of land which is equivalent to area of New Delhi by 2050. Thermocol and plastic waste are two biggest components of landfills and both households and Industries generate such waste. Waste generated from household becomes a problem for Municipal Corporations and disposal of Industrial waste becomes a matter of worry for the management.

About Thermowaste Solutions


Thermocol waste generated by both households and industries is recycled by Micro Gartex Industries under its Swachh Bharat initiative Thermowaste Solutions so as to reduce the area occupied by landfills. Best efforts are made to save land for the future generations because we believe that, We do not inherit this planet from our ancestors, we have borrowed it from the coming generation that too on full repair lease.
